About Me

Hello! I’m Natalia Mwangi, a Computer Science student at Strathmore University, currently in the last semester of my third year. I’m passionate about technology and its potential to create meaningful solutions in our lives. I’m particularly interested in building applications that can improve people's quality of life and fullfillment.

In addition to my studies, I’ve been actively involved in various personal and group projects, including web development, Android app development, and machine learning. I enjoy tackling challenges, whether it’s configuring a complex system or building user-friendly applications. When I’m not coding, I’m often brainstorming new ideas, reading up on tech trends, or finding ways to integrate design and technology.

Outside of school and tech, I’m always looking for opportunities to give back to my community, especially through initiatives that support them. I'm excited about the future and the impact technology can have in making the world a better place.
