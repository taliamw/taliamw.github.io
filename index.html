About Me

Hello! I’m Natalia Mwangi, a Computer Science student at Strathmore University, currently in the last semester of my third year. I’m passionate about technology and its potential to create meaningful solutions, especially for individuals with disabilities. I’m particularly interested in building accessible applications that can improve the lives of people with unique needs, which is why I developed Sauti, a speech-to-text and text-to-speech app that won a recent hackathon focused on people with disabilities. In addition to my studies, I’ve been actively involved in various personal and group projects, including web development, Android app development, and machine learning. I enjoy tackling challenges, whether it’s configuring a complex system or building user-friendly applications. When I’m not coding, I’m often brainstorming new ideas, reading up on tech trends, or finding ways to integrate design and technology. Outside of school and tech, I’m always looking for opportunities to give back to my community, especially through initiatives that support people with autism and other sensory sensitivities. I'm excited about the future and the impact technology can have in making the world more inclusive and accessible.

My Services

Web Design

I have a strong foundation in web design, with an emphasis on creating visually engaging, responsive, and user-friendly websites. I’m proficient in designing layouts that enhance user experience (UX) and user interface (UI), utilizing tools such as Figma, and other design software to craft clean, modern designs.

My experience includes designing event posters and promotional materials, which has allowed me to sharpen my skills in visual storytelling and presenting information in a clear, impactful way. I strive to blend creative design with technical precision, making sure that the end product not only looks good but also performs well in terms of usability.

Web Development

I have hands-on experience in web development, working with both front-end and back-end technologies. I’m skilled in building dynamic and user-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I’ve worked with MySQL databases to store and manage data, ensuring efficient data handling and secure user authentication.

In addition to PHP, I’ve also explored front-end frameworks and worked with APIs to integrate external data and enhance user interactions on the web. I’m always eager to learn new tools and techniques to improve my development skills and create seamless, responsive web applications.

Poster Design

I specialize in creating visually impactful posters that effectively communicate key messages. I focus on typography, color theory, and visual hierarchy to ensure the design captures attention while conveying information clearly. Each poster is tailored to the target audience, ensuring relevance and accessibility.

I craft designs for various events, promotions, and campaigns. Whether it's for academic events or community outreach, my goal is to produce designs that are both creative and functional, making them effective in their purpose.

Mobile App Development

I am experienced in developing mobile applications, focusing on creating user-friendly, efficient, and functional apps. I have worked with Kotlin to build Android apps, utilizing modern tools and technologies like Jetpack Compose to create seamless user interfaces and integrate features such as Speech-to-Text and Text-to-Speech. I prioritize delivering a smooth user experience, ensuring apps are responsive and intuitive. I also have experience in designing and implementing features that cater to specific needs, such as accessibility, making the app more inclusive for diverse users.

WordPress Dev
